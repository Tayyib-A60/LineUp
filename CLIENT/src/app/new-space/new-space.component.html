This warm & creative space is within Present Story, a beautiful gift shop located on Nicholson Street in Coburg. The space measures 6 metres x 3.5 metres and tables and seating for 8 is provided, or the space can be cleared for specific use. Seagrass floor, heating/cooling, and speaker system make for an intimate meeting space, area for women's circles, workshops, small gatherings, exhibition space, classes, or brand/retail pop up. Present Story is open to the public from 10am to 6pm Tuesday to Friday, 10am - 5pm Saturday and 10am - 4pm Sunday. Outside these hours, the space is completely private.

This space has been created to run local, community arts & crafts workshops on weekends. As such, many weekends will be booked for this purpose but the space is also available during the week and evenings by arrangement.

Minimum one hour bookings for off peak periods, with weekend bookings requiring a minimum 3 hour booking. We are also open to discussing reduced pricing for community based not for profit events.

Present Story is an inclusive space and welcomes all sections of our diverse community.

Our website currently reflects our retail space only so please drop in or contact Linda to discuss a viewing of the space at a time that suits you.
